Travel Insurance

Protect your journey with our comprehensive travel insurance plans.

1. Why Choose Travel Insurance?

Travel insurance provides peace of mind by covering unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. It’s an essential safeguard for any traveler.

2. Coverage Details

  • Trip Cancellation/Interruption: Reimbursement for non-refundable costs if you cancel or cut your trip short.
  • Medical Emergencies: Coverage for hospital stays, doctor visits, and emergency evacuation.
  • Lost or Delayed Baggage: Compensation for lost, stolen, or delayed luggage.
  • Travel Delays: Reimbursement for expenses due to flight delays or missed connections.

3. How to Purchase

You can add travel insurance during the booking process on our website. Select your plan, review the details, and pay the additional premium online.

4. Eligibility and Exclusions

Insurance is available to all travelers, but coverage may exclude pre-existing medical conditions, high-risk activities (e.g., extreme sports), or travel to restricted areas. Please review the policy terms.

5. Claims Process

To file a claim, contact us within 30 days of the incident. Submit required documentation (e.g., medical reports, receipts) via email to claims@exploremore.com.
